Interface to Atmospheric Physics radiation code.

Interface to Atmospheric Physics radiation code. Purpose: This is the top-level radiation control routine. Major book-keeping is carried out here. Separate calls are made to calculate SW and LW radiative fluxes and heating rates on radiation time-steps. Radiation increments are applied on all physics time-steps.
