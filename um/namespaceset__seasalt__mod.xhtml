Detailed Description

Subroutine to calculate sea-salt aerosol number concentration. Subroutine to calculate sea-salt aerosol number concentration.

Purpose: Sea-salt aerosol number concentration in 2 modes is calculated from windspeed, height above surface and land fraction. Method: 10m windspeed is used to calculate sea-salt aerosol number concentration in 2 modes following O'Dowd et al. (1999). An exponential decay with height of these concentrations is then assumed, and they are scaled depending on the fraction of open water in the gridbox.
