Subroutine Rcf_smc_stress - calculate the soil moisture stress.

This module calculate soil moisture stress fields, and then interpolate smc_stress into the new domain. This smc_stress will be converted into the soil moisture in the post process routine. !> Method: Read input of smc, smc at wilting and critical point. Calculate smc stress using these inputs and then interpolate smc_stress to the new domain. The smc in the new domain will be then calculated from smc_stress in the post process routine.
