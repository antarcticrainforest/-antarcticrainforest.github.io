Module providing UKCA with inputs describing standard tropospheric chemistry for the ASAD system. Will configure tropospheric chemistry to include aerosol chemistry and isoprene chemistry as required. The subroutine INIT_STD_TROP constructs the final arrays from the components using logicals defined in the UMUI. Part of the UKCA model, a community model supported by the Met Office and NCAS, with components provided initially by The University of Cambridge, University of Leeds and The Met. Office.
