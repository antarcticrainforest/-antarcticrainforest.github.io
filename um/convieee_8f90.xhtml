SUBROUTINE CONVIEEE!> Purpose: Converts a dump, ancillary or fieldsfile (atmosphere or ocean) between different into 32-bit or 64-bit IEEE format or vice-versa. The following conversions are supported:!> On a IEEE machine 64-bit IEEE to 32-bit IEEE 64-bit IEEE to 64-bit IEEE In either case, WGDOS data will be unpacked if requested. CONVIEEE reads in headers and data fields from unit NFTIN converts them to IEEE format and writes them to NFTOUT.
