LL SUBROUTINE VANRAIN!>LL LL Purpose : Performs 'vertical' analysis for precip rate/phase data. LL LL When IPASS=1, LL model field is interpolated to ob locations and increments LL calculated. Preliminary weight normalisation factors are also LL calculated. LL LL When IPASS=2, LL data density is interpolated to ob locations and LL final weight normalisation factors are calculated.
