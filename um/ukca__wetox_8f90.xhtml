[Met Office Ref SC138] Calculates conversion of dissolved SO2 to sulfate by reaction with H2O2 and O3. Uses Henry's law constants & oxidation rate constants from Seinfeld & Pandis "Atmospheric Chemistry & Physics" Note that this code is for use in the CTM and box model framework or where the SO2 wet oxidation is to be done in the aerosol code (WETOX_IN_AER=1). UKCA is a community model supported by The Met Office and NCAS, with components initially provided by The University of Cambridge, University of Leeds and The Met Office.
