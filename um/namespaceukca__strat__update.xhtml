UKCA stratospheric photolysis main routine. Test version

Subroutine Interface: Subroutine STRAT_PHOTOL This routine computes stratospheric photolysis rates and merges the rates, where necessary, with the tropospheric rates. This is done for one level at a time. The stratospheric photolysis routines are taken from SLIMCAT.
