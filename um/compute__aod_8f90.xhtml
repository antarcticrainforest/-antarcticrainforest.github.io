Subroutine to compute the aerosol optical depth (AOD).

Subroutine to compute the aerosol optical depth (AOD). Method: The optical depth is the vertical integral of the aerosol specific extinction weighted by the aerosol mass. This file belongs in section: Radiation Control FORTRAN 77

Parameters
n_aerosolarray dimensions (actual and fixed)
n_aerosol_mrnumber of aerosol components in spectral information
npd_aerosolnumber of aerosol components in mixing ration information
n_aod_wavelnumber of wavelengths at which the AOD is to be computed
n_profilenumber of grid-boxes
n_layernumber of vertical layers
n_humiditiesnumber of humidities used in moist aerosol parameterisation
type_aerosolarray giving the type of each aerosol component (see AERCMP3A)
i_aod_typearray connecting an aerosol component to an aerosol type
i_type_wantedaerosol type to be considered in this call
aerosol_mix_ratioaerosol component mass mixing ratio
aerosol_mr_type_indexIndex relating aerosol_mix_ratio aerosols to aerosols in the spectral information

i_aerosol_parametrizationaerosol parameterisation (dry or moist)
aod_absorptionaerosol specific coefficients for absorption and scattering (monochromatic)
