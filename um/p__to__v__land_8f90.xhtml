subroutine P_TO_V_LAND for calculating land variables held at

subroutine P_TO_V_LAND for calculating land variables held at p points at v points, on both the new dynamics and endgame grids It should be noted that in endgame scenario the p pts either!> side of the pole need not necessarily be both land points.!> This routine does not yet cater for when one is land and the other sea. This routine does interior points of array not halos, but requires halo information to be set.
