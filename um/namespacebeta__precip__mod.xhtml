SUBROUTINE BETA_PRECIP -!> PURPOSE: SUBROUTINE BETA_PRECIP -!> PURPOSE: Process fields of precipitation intensity to give scattering coefft in 1/metres. Calculated at model level (eg bottom eta level 25m) or level within surface layer eg screen ht ( 1.5M ) Programming standard: U M Doc. Paper No. 4.
