Parameters (in future consider putting in a module).

Determine shape of mass flux profile. If the depth of convection below the zero degree level is large enough, and the depth above is as well, the mass flux profile is assumed to have a maximum at the zero degree level.

Mass flux profile has an elevated maximum, the value of the maximum mass flux increase with decreasing pressure of the zero degree level while it is below p_ref. Above p_ref the maximum value is fixed.
