Sol'n of inhomogeneous Rayleigh scattering atmosphere. (original Rayleigh w/ polarization) Cochran and Trafton, 1978, Ap.J., 219, 756-762. Raman scattering in the atmospheres of the major planets. (first use of anisotropic code) Jacob, Gottlieb and Prather, 1989, J.Geophys.Res., 94, 12975-13002.
