Module to define fields from D1 needed for UKCA these are set in UKCA_SETD1DEFS Part of the UKCA model. UKCA is a community model supported by The Met Office and NCAS, with components provided initially by The University of Cambridge, University of Leeds and The Met. Office. See www.ukca.ac.uk Method: This file belongs in section: UKCA Language: FORTRAN 90 ######################################################################
