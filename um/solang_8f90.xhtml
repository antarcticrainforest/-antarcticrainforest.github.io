Calculation of solar zenith/hour angles and sunlit fraction.

Calculation of solar zenith/hour angles and sunlit fraction. Purpose : Calculations of the earth's orbit described in the second page of the "Calculation of incoming insolation" section of UMDP 23, i.e. from the sin of the solar declination, the position of each point and the time limits it calculates how much sunlight, if any, it receives.
