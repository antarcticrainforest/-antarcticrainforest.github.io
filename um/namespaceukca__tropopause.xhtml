Detailed Description

Purpose: Module containing subroutine to calculate the pressure of.

Purpose: Module containing subroutine to calculate the pressure of the 2.0pvu surface and the pressure of the 380K surface. Routine combines them to calculate the pressure of the tropopause and set L_troposphere to .true. for those!> gridboxes in the troposphere. Part of the UKCA model, a community model supported by The Met Office and NCAS, with components provided initially by The University of Cambridge, University of Leeds and The Met. Office.
