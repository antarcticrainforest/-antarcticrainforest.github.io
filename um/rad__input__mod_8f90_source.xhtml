163 LOGICAL :: l_rad_ovrlap = .false. ! Requires l_ccrad=.TRUE.
164 ! Allows Convective and LS Cloud to overlap! for radiative impacts.
165 ! (Experimental, defaulted to FALSE,
166 ! requires a hand-edit to change)
167 ! (THIS IS EXPERIMENTAL AND USED FOR DEVELOPMENT ONLY).
168 ! Current convective/large-scale cloud fractions in the
169 ! radiation scheme are mutally exclusive. This assumes CCA
170 ! and the large-scale to overlap with CCA taking dominance.
171 ! I.E. Large-scale cloud fraction must exceed the convective
172 ! cloud fraction before having any presence.
173 
174 LOGICAL :: l_rad_ccw_scav = .false.
175 ! Requires l_ccrad=.TRUE. .AND. l_rad_ovrlap=.TRUE.
176 ! Allows Convective Cloud Water (CCW) to
177 ! compensate for LS Cloud water in overlapping
178 ! LS/CCA fractions.
179 ! (Experimental, defaulted to FALSE, requires a
180 ! hand-edit to change)
181 ! (THIS IS EXPERIMENTAL AND USED FOR DEVELOPMENT ONLY)
182 ! Allowing the CCA to negate large-scale fractions of lower
183 ! values means that the large-scale cloud water in the
184 ! overlapping fraction is lost. This switch will scavenge
185 ! the large-scale cloud water from the overlapping fraction
186 ! and combine it with the convective cloud water to
187 ! conpensate.
