Method: Required level lists and logical switches are determined by the calling routine from STASH requests and STASHflags. Intercepted arrays - calculated unconditionally - and diagnostic arrays - dependent on STASHflags - are input from the large scale precipitation routines called previously. Each diagnostic is simply copied into the STASHwork array to be passed on to STASH for output processing, except where indicated.

Diagnostics currently available: (in order calculated) STASH items (all section 21) !> 100: lightning flash rate (s-1) 101: Storm location flag 102: Graupel Water Path (GWP; kg m-2) 103: Total Ice Water Path (TIWP; kg m-2) 104: Total number of flashes 105: Flash rate from graupel flux (McCaul Scheme) 106: Flash rate from storm ice (McCaul Scheme)
