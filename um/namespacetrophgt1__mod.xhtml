◆ z_min_trop

real, parameter trophgt1_mod::z_min_trop = 2.0e3

radiative calculations are defined.

  • !> Set limits on the height of the tropopause. Generous limits are required as the tropopause can be very low in the Antarctic winter. These limits should be reviewed for simulations of climates very different from the present one, such as runs with very high concentrations of CO2. These limits are in part chosen to accord with the pressure levels used in earlier configurations of the Unified Model. In setting the upper limit consideration has been given to the paper entitled "The tropical tropopause over the Western Pacifiic: Wave driving, convection and the annual cycle," (J. Geophys. Res., 1996, Vol. 101 (D16), p. 21223) by G. C. Reid and K. S. Gage.
