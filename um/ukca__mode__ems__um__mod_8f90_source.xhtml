164 ! Now use ISO2EMS=1 when chosen only sulphate and sea-salt in 4 modes
165 ! (no BC/OC) -- i_mode_setup=1 -- small sizes
166 ! make up for lack of primary BC/OC emissions
167 ! For all other options use ISO2EMS=3 as standard as in GLOMAP.
168 ! N.B. parfrac variable now determines fraction of SO2 emissions as SO4.
169 !
170 ! ISO2EMS == 1 : 3.0% of SO2 ems --> primary SO4 ems --> 15%/85% to
171 ! 10/70 nm g.m.diam. modes as in Spracklen (2005)
172 ! and Binkowski & Shankar (1995).
173 !
174 ! ISO2EMS == 2 : 2.5% of SO2 ems --> primary SO4 ems
175 ! road/off-road/domestic all --> 30nm gm.diam mode
176 ! industrial/power-plant/ship all --> 1000nm gm.diam mode
177 ! as for original AEROCOM size recommendations.
178 !
179 ! ISO2EMS >= 3 : 2.5% of SO2 ems --> primary SO4 ems --> 50%/50% to
180 ! 150/1500nm g.m.diam. modes as for Stier et al (2005)
181 ! modified AEROCOM sizdis recommendations.
182 !
183 ! Note also that currently, ISO2EMS also controls size assumptions for
184 ! primary carbonaceous aerosol (this needs to be changed):
185 !
186 ! ISO2EMS /= 3 : biofuel & biomass BC/OC emissions --> 80nm g.m.diam.
187 ! fossil-fuel BC/OC emissions --> 30nm g.m.diam.
188 !
189 ! ISO2EMS == 3 : biofuel & biomass BC/OC emissions --> 150nm g.m.diam.
