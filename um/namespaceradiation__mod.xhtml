◆ cmessage

character(len=256) radiation_mod::cmessage

Finds atmospheric heating due to longwave radiation. Assumes that absorption is a function only of pressure; the moisture content of the atmosphere is completely disregarded.

Method: Uses a Runge-Kutta solver to find the upward and downward longwave radiation fluxes subject to the boundary conditions that there is no downward flux at the top of the atmosphere and the upward flux at the bottoms of the atmosphere is that of a black body at the surface temperature. It would perhaps be more accurate to work in variables of U+D and U-D, where U and D are the upward and downward fluxes respectively, since U-D is what we want at the end; but this would complicate the boundary conditions such that it would no longer be a simple initial value problem, so this is not done.
