Parameters
[in]xMethod: Fits a cubic to the interval between two points containing h0, by matching the values and derivatives of x at the gridpoints. The derivatives of x at gridpoint N is estimated by taking the gradients of straight lines joining gridpoints N-1 to N, and N to N+1. If this is not possible because there are not enough gridpoints, the averaging is not done.
