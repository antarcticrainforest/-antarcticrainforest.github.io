 SUBROUTINE BDY_LAYR- SUBROUTINE BDY_LAYR- Purpose: Calculate turbulent fluxes of heat, moisture and momentum between (a) surface and atmosphere, (b) atmospheric levels within the boundary layer, and/or the effects of these fluxes on the primary model variables. The flux of heat into and through the soil is also modelled. Numerous related diagnostics are also calculated. More...
