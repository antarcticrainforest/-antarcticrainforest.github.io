Calculates condensation of condensable cpt vapours onto pre-existing aerosol particles. Includes switch for using either Fuchs (1964) or modified Fuchs and Sutugin (1971) calculation of CC. UKCA is a community model supported by The Met Office and NCAS, with components initially provided by The University of Cambridge, University of Leeds and The Met Office.
